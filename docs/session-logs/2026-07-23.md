## Why we reverted (the honest call)

Even with top-down poses + continuity seeding, **MoveIt/KDL is not delivering a
usable scan trajectory for this overhead-mounted arm** without significant further
work: the top-down raster is validated only in sim (not on hardware), the free-space
bridges between scan lines still fail (0/8), camera-offset-exact aiming is unbuilt,
and KDL's branch instability likely needs TracIK/bio-ik or a host-side
branch-controlled IK. Rather than keep risking the real arm mid-debug, we reverted to
the console approach that **already drove the real arm acceptably** (aim-down,
controller IK), and preserved the ROS effort intact on `internal-ik-ros`.
