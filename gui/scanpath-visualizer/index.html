The arm plays the imported path back: it hangs from an overhead mount at the table centre and its scanner tracks each waypoint. With X-slider on it rides a gantry along X; off, the base is fixed. Poses out of reach are shown held/dimmed. No collision checking (the real CAD isn't loaded here).
